FAQ

Common questions

Does Free give me the full review experience?
switchman review — including the full session summary, semantic overlap detection, interface mismatch flagging, and merge confidence score — is free forever with no limits. Pro adds the over-time layer on top: history, team sharing, and cross-session intelligence.
What specifically am I paying for?
The over-time layer. 90-day searchable session history, cross-session pattern detection (which parts of your codebase consistently flag amber), team session sharing, AI task planning informed by your conflict history, and cost tracking across sessions.
Why does the history limit matter?
Free keeps 14 days — enough for active development. The moment you want to look back at a session from three weeks ago to understand why a file is structured a certain way, or share context with a new teammate, Pro's 90-day history is what you need.
Are notifications free?
Yes. Slack and desktop notifications are available on both Free and Pro.
Does Pro require a constant internet connection?
No. Licence checks cache locally for 24 hours. Pro works offline for up to 7 days. History sync needs connectivity when it writes; local coordination runs fine without it.
Will this change my git workflow?
No. Switchman coordinates around git. It does not replace it. Your branches, history, and remotes stay exactly as they are.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel through Polar.sh at any time. Your local SQLite data and session history are always yours and are not affected by cancellation.
Is this safe for company code?
Yes. Switchman coordinates metadata — task state, file claims, session summaries. It does not upload your source code. All file operations stay local.
Who is Pro really for?
Developers who've seen the value of Free and now want the over-time layer — searchable history, team visibility, and a system that gets smarter about their specific codebase. Also anyone who needs to show a tech lead or manager what the agents actually built and whether it was safe to ship.
